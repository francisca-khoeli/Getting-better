HOW DO YOU KNOW ITS NEGATIVE?

Negative peer pressure pushes you to the worst.

When you give in to peer pressure,you often feel guilty or disappointed with yourself for acting against your values.

Someone, either your friend or part of the group you belong to may make you feel like you have to do something to be accepted.

As a result,you individually land yourself in deep sorrow and feel remorseful about the whole situation, you lose your lifestyle and entirely adopt your peer's living.

Furthermore, you lose your identity surrending to peer pressure.
