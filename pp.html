PEER PRESSURE ISN'T ALL BAD!

Usually peer presure is used in a negative context, but there is always another positive side of it.

Peer pressure can leave a positive impact on your life if you carefully pick certain good habits from your peers.

Good peer pressure is when you get pushed into something that you didn't want to do and it turned out well.

FOR INSTANCE;Your peers may teach you some good things about life and encourage you to follow them.

EFFECTS OF POSITIVE PEER PRESSURE

  1. SENSE OF BELONGING

    2. Your peers may help you with your studies,push you harder to do better in important tasks and motivate you,

    this helps you to create a sense of belonging with your peers.

  2. ADVICE AND ENCOURAGEMEMT
