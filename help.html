WAYS TO DEAL WITH PEER PRESSURE

  1. SCHOOLS SHOULD ADDRESS THIS TOPIC

    2. Students should be educated about peer pressure.

    Through this, children become prepared to abstain from peer pressure actions.

  2. FORMATION OF FRIENDSHIPS WITH THE RIGHT PEOPLE

    3. People who value you are less likely to bully you into doing things you do not want to do,

    they only want the best from you.

  3. HAVE CONVERSATIONS WITH TRUSTED ADULTS

    4. Identify adults you feel safe and comfortable around ,by so doing,

    you will feel free to communicate with them and they might help you with tricky situations.

"NEVER BE BULLIED INTO SILENCE. NEVER ALLOW YOURSELF TO BE MADE A VICTIM.

ACCEPT NO ONE'S DEFINATION OF YOUR LIFE, BUT DEFINE YOURSELF"
